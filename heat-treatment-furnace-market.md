<p><p>The heat treatment furnace market is primarily divided into atmosphere furnaces and vacuum furnaces. Atmosphere furnaces utilize controlled atmospheres to prevent oxidation and contamination during heat treatment processes, making them ideal for various applications. Vacuum furnaces operate in a vacuum environment, eliminating atmospheric gases and providing superior quality and consistency in heat treatment, particularly for high-performance materials. Both types are essential in industries like automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, offering tailored solutions for enhancing material properties.</p></p>

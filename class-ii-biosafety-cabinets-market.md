<p><p>Class II Biosafety Cabinets are designed to provide a sterile environment for handling biological materials. The market is segmented into three main types:</p><p>A Type cabinets recirculate both air and provide some level of exhaust, suitable for work with non-volatile agents. B Type cabinets feature a combination of recirculated and discharged air, ideal for working with volatile agents. C Type cabinets, also known as ducted cabinets, continuously discharge air, offering enhanced protection for hazardous materials. Each type addresses specific safety and containment needs in laboratory settings.</p></p>

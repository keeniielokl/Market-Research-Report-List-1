<p><strong>The Dental Gypsum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dental Plaster</li><li>Model Dental Stone</li><li>Die Dental Stone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental gypsum market is categorized into three main types: dental plaster, model dental stone, and die dental stone. Dental plaster is primarily used for preliminary models and can be easily manipulated, making it ideal for general applications. Model dental stone offers higher strength and enhanced detail reproduction, commonly used for creating precise dental models. Die dental stone is the most durable, designed for producing accurate replicas of prepared teeth, essential for crown and bridge work in dental practices.</p></p>

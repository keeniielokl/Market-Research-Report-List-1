<p><p>The non-contact temperature sensors market includes infrared temperature sensors and fiber optic temperature sensors. Infrared temperature sensors detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, allowing for rapid and precise temperature measurements without physical contact. They are widely used in industrial applications and medical diagnostics. Fiber optic temperature sensors utilize light transmitted through optical fibers, detecting temperature changes based on variations in light properties. These sensors are valued for their ability to function in harsh environments and provide high accuracy in demanding applications.</p></p>

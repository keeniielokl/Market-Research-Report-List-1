<p><p>The oil-water separation purifier market is primarily categorized into two types: hydraulic type and sealed type. Hydraulic type purifiers utilize mechanical forces to facilitate the separation of oil from water, making them suitable for larger-scale applications. In contrast, sealed type purifiers are designed to operate in a closed system, minimizing exposure to the environment and enhancing safety. Both types aim to effectively remove contaminants from water, addressing environmental concerns and regulatory compliance in various industries.</p></p>

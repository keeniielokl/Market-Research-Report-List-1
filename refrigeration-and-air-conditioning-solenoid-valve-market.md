<p><p>The refrigeration and air conditioning solenoid valve market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Direct-Acting Type**: These valves operate directly via the electromagnetic force, making them suitable for low-pressure applications.</p><p>2. **Sub-step Direct-Acting Type**: A variation of direct-acting valves, these offer enhanced control by managing multiple flow steps, improving efficiency.</p><p>3. **Pilot-Operated Type**: These valves utilize a pilot pressure to control a larger fluid flow, ideal for high-pressure systems, offering robust performance and energy efficiency. </p><p>Each type serves specific applications within HVAC systems.</p></p>

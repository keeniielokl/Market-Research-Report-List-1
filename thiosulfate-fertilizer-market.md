<p><p>The thiosulfate fertilizer market is divided into two main types: liquid and solid fertilizers. Liquid thiosulfate fertilizers are easily absorbed by plants, providing rapid nutrient delivery and facilitating foliar application. They enhance nutrient uptake while minimizing leaching. Solid thiosulfate fertilizers, on the other hand, are preferable for bulk application and slow release, improving soil structure and nutrient retention. Both forms support enhanced crop growth and yield, catering to diverse agricultural practices and soil conditions.</p></p>

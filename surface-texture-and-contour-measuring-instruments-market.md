<p><p>The surface texture and contour measuring instruments market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact measuring instruments. Contact instruments utilize physical probes to touch the surface, providing precise measurements of texture and contour through direct interaction. In contrast, non-contact instruments employ advanced technologies like laser and optical systems to gauge surface features without physical contact, allowing for quick assessments and the ability to measure delicate surfaces without damage. Each type caters to specific applications and industry needs.</p></p>

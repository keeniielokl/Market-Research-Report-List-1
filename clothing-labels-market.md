<p><p>The clothing labels market comprises three main types: woven, damask, and printed labels. Woven labels are made by weaving threads together, offering durability and a high-quality appearance, ideal for premium brands. Damask labels are a variant of woven labels, featuring intricate designs and a soft texture, commonly used in high-end clothing. Printed labels, on the other hand, are created by printing ink onto fabric, allowing for vibrant designs but generally less durability, making them suitable for various applications, including promotional apparel.</p></p>

<p><p>The cloth diaper market consists of several types, primarily pocket diapers, fitted diapers, and all-in-ones. Pocket diapers feature a waterproof outer layer with a pocket to insert absorbent inserts, allowing for customization of absorbency. Fitted diapers are absorbent and contoured for shape but require a separate cover for waterproofing. All-in-ones combine absorbency and waterproofing in a single unit, offering convenience and ease of use. Each type caters to different parenting preferences and lifestyles, contributing to the overall appeal of cloth diapering.</p></p>

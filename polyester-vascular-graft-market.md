<p><p>The polyester vascular graft market is segmented into low porosity and high porosity types. Low porosity grafts have a dense structure, minimizing blood leakage and promoting thrombus formation, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. High porosity grafts feature more openings, enhancing tissue integration and promoting cell growth, ideal for long-term vascular replacement. These distinctions cater to varied clinical needs, with low porosity offering strength and reliability, while high porosity focuses on biocompatibility and effective healing.</p></p>

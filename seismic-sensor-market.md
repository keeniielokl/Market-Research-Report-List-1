<p><strong>The Seismic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inertial Seismometer</li><li>Extensometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seismic sensor market encompasses various types, notably inertial seismometers and extensometers. Inertial seismometers measure ground motion by detecting changes in inertia, making them ideal for earthquake monitoring and structural health assessment. Extensometers, on the other hand, gauge ground deformation by measuring the distance between points, crucial for analyzing soil stability and subsurface movements. Both technologies play essential roles in geophysical research, construction safety, and natural disaster prediction, reflecting a growing demand for accurate monitoring solutions in various applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The Integrated Video Surveillance System market primarily consists of two main types: Network Video Recorder (NVR) systems and Digital Video Recorder (DVR) systems. NVR systems utilize IP cameras, providing high-resolution video storage and remote access via network connections, making them ideal for modern, scalable applications. In contrast, DVR systems rely on analog cameras and coaxial cables, offering a more straightforward setup but limited flexibility. Both systems serve to enhance security, monitoring, and data management in various environments.</p></p>

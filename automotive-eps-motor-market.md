<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive EPS (Electric Power Steering) motor market includes several types: </p><p>1. **CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering)** motors are integrated within the steering column, providing direct and responsive steering feedback.</p><p>2. **REPS (Rack Electric Power Steering)** motors are mounted on the steering rack, enhancing efficiency and offering a more compact design.</p><p>3. **PEPS (Pintent Electric Power Steering)** motors feature a dual design that connects both the steering column and rack, improving adaptability.</p><p>4. **EHPS (Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering)** combines electric and hydraulic systems, offering a performance-focused solution for high-demand vehicles.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Airport RFID System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airport RFID System market consists of three primary types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF). LF systems, typically operating between 125 kHz and 134 kHz, are suitable for tracking assets over short distances. HF systems, ranging from 13.56 MHz, offer better read ranges and are often used for baggage tracking. UHF systems, operating between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, provide the longest read ranges and are ideal for high-volume operations, enhancing efficiency in airport logistics.</p></p>

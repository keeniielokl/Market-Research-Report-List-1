<p><p>Color coated sheets for household appliances are specialized metal sheets treated with color coatings to enhance aesthetics and durability. The primary market types include Polyester Color Coated Metal (PCM) and Vinyl Coated Metal (VCM). PCM offers a smooth finish with excellent weather resistance, making it suitable for fridges and washing machines. VCM, on the other hand, provides a textured appearance and increased chemical resistance, ideal for appliances exposed to heat or chemicals. Both enhance the visual appeal and longevity of household products.</p></p>

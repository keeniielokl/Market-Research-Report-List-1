<p><strong>The Automotive PVD Coating Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation Coating</li><li>Sputter Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating service market encompasses various processes, notably evaporation coating and sputter coating. Evaporation coating involves vaporizing a material under vacuum, allowing it to condense on the substrate, creating thin films with desirable properties. Sputter coating, on the other hand, utilizes a plasma to dislodge atoms from a target material, depositing them onto the vehicle surface. Both methods enhance aesthetics and durability, providing protective layers that improve performance and longevity in automotive applications.</p></p>

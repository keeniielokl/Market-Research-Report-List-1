<p><p>The IC fabrication equipment market encompasses various types essential for semiconductor manufacturing. Microlithography and mask making equipment are crucial for patterning circuits on substrates. CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) equipment ensures wafer surface uniformity. Ion implanters embed dopants into silicon wafers, while deposition tools apply thin films of materials. Etching and cleaning tools shape and purify surfaces, and process diagnostic equipment monitors conditions to maintain quality. Collectively, these technologies enable the precise and efficient production of integrated circuits.</p></p>

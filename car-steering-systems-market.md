<p><p>Car steering systems are essential for vehicle control, categorized mainly into mechanical and power steering systems. Mechanical steering systems rely on direct physical linkage between the steering wheel and wheels, offering simplicity and lower maintenance. In contrast, power steering systems enhance maneuverability through hydraulic or electric assistance, reducing driver effort and improving control, especially at low speeds. The market for these systems varies, with increasing preference for power steering due to its comfort and precision, adapting to modern vehicle demands.</p></p>

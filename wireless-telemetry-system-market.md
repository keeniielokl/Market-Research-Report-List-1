<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless telemetry system market is divided into two primary types: single-channel and multi-channel. Single-channel systems transmit data through one communication path, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, multi-channel systems can send and receive data across multiple paths simultaneously, allowing for greater data throughput and more complex monitoring capabilities. This versatility makes multi-channel systems suitable for applications requiring simultaneous data collection from various sensors, enhancing efficiency and reliability in telemetry operations.</p></p>

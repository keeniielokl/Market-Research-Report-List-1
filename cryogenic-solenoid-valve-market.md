<p><strong>The Cryogenic Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger Valve</li><li>Direct-Acting Plunger Valve</li><li>Pilot-Operated Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic solenoid valve market encompasses three main types: plunger valves, direct-acting plunger valves, and pilot-operated valves. Plunger valves utilize an electromechanical plunger to control flow, ideal for low-pressure applications. Direct-acting plunger valves respond directly to electrical signals, providing precise flow control in cryogenic systems. Pilot-operated valves, on the other hand, use a small pilot valve to manage larger flow rates, offering enhanced efficiency for high-pressure environments. Each type caters to specific operational needs within cryogenic applications.</p></p>

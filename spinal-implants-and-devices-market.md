<p><p>The spinal implants and devices market is primarily divided into fusion devices and spinal biologics. Fusion devices are surgical implants designed to stabilize the spine, promoting bone growth between vertebrae to reduce pain and enhance mobility. Common types include spinal cages, rods, and screws. Spinal biologics, on the other hand, involve biological materials and substances that aid in tissue regeneration and healing, such as bone grafts and growth factors, supporting spinal surgery recovery and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>

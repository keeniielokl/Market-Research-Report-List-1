<p><strong>The AF Driver IC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Driver IC</li><li>Close Loop Driver IC</li><li>OIS Driver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AF Driver IC market includes three main types: Open Loop Driver ICs, Close Loop Driver ICs, and OIS Driver ICs. Open Loop Driver ICs operate without feedback, delivering simpler designs and lower costs but less accuracy. Close Loop Driver ICs, with feedback mechanisms, provide precise control for autofocus systems, enhancing image quality. OIS Driver ICs are specifically designed for optical image stabilization, minimizing blurriness in photos and videos. Each type caters to specific performance needs in camera applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The tire recycling line market encompasses three main product types: rubber blocks, colloidal rubber, and rubber powder. Rubber blocks are large, solid forms of recycled rubber used in various applications like flooring and construction. Colloidal rubber is a fine, liquid-like form utilized in adhesives and sealants. Rubber powder, produced from grinding tires, serves as a raw material in manufacturing processes and is integrated into products like asphalt and playground surfaces. Each type caters to different industrial needs and applications.</p></p>

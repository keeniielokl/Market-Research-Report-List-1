<p><p>The car tachograph market is divided into two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built directly into the vehicle's dashboard, providing real-time data on driving behavior, speed, and distance traveled. They offer seamless functionality and are often used in fleet management systems. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are standalone devices that can be easily moved between vehicles. They offer flexibility for monitoring driving patterns and are ideal for smaller operators or occasional use, ensuring compliance with regulations.</p></p>
